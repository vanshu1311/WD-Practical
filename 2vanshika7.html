Dr. A.P.J Abdul Kalam

Missile Man of India 🚀

About

Dr. A.P.J Abdul Kalam was a renowned Indian scientist and the 11th President of India. He played a vital role in India's space and missile development programs and inspired millions with his vision for a developed India.

Achievements

  • Bharat Ratna Awardee
  • Key figure in Pokhran-II nuclear tests
  • Developed India's first satellite launch vehicle (SLV-III)
  • Served as President of India (2002–2007)

Timeline

1931: Born in Rameswaram

1960: Joined DRDO

1980: SLV-III Success

2002: Became President

2015: Passed away inspiring students
